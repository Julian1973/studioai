FADE IN:  EXT. BUZZING NOOK – MORNING                             1 Golden morning light. Dew sparkles on the leaves. A golden HONEYCOMB glistens from a branch. The forest is quiet. Still. Perfect. CAMERA PULLS BACK to reveal a patch of flowers below the honeycomb. One flower… BOUNCES slightly. Then again. Moving closer to the honeycomb. Very slowly. Very carefully. It!s KEEN — disguised as a flower. Petals around his head. Leaves stuck to his arms. He inches closer. Almost there, Fuzzby fly’s past. Keen FREEZES. One eye twitching. Fuzzby stops. Double takes. Flies back. Tilts his head at the flower. Sniffs it. The flower has PAWS. Fuzzby reaches out. Plucks a petal.  1 FUZZBY She loves me…  He plucks another.  2 FUZZBY (CONT!D) She loves me not…  Another. Faster now.  3 FUZZBY (CONT!D) She loves me — she loves me not —  Petals fall rapidly. Keen holds it together. Barely. His eye twitches harder. His paw curls into a fist. The last petal drops. Just Keen. Standing in a pile of petals. Completely still. Fuzzby looks at the pile of petals. Then at Keen. Then back at the petals. A long beat.  4 FUZZBY (CONT!D) (genuinely curious) Were you a flower?  5 KEEN (through gritted teeth) Yes Fuzzby. I was a flower.  Fuzzby nods slowly. Taking that in. 
 
 
 6 FUZZBY You weren!t very convincing.  Keen stares at him.  7 KEEN I know.  Fuzzby pats him on the shoulder sympathetically. Then buzzes off cheerfully. Keen watches him go. Sighs. He looks at the honeycomb. Still untouched. Still perfect. Still completely out of reach. He sighs again and walks away.  CUT TO:  EXT. CRYSTAL COVE BEACH – MORNING                     2 Soft waves roll onto the shore. The sand glows warm in the early light. AIDA walks slowly along the waterline. Her ROSE QUARTZ PENDANT glows softly. She pauses. Looks down. Hidden paw prints in the wet sand. Small. Uneven. Going in circles. Back and forth. Alone. Aida kneels beside them. Traces one gently with her paw. Her pendant pulses.  AIDA!S INNER VISION — THROUGH AIDA!S EYES  A small fox cub. Alone in the dark. Turning in circles. Lost. Frightened. Looking for something she cannot find.  BACK TO AIDA - The glow fades from her eyes. She places a paw over her heart.  8 AIDA Someone has been here a long time. All on their own.  She rises and heads back toward the others.  CUT TO:  EXT. CRYSTAL COVE BEACH – CONTINUOUS                  3 The bears walk together along the shoreline. Relaxed. Easy. Happy. FUZZBY zig-zags ahead, narrating his own adventure to nobody in particular. KEEN follows, 
 
 
kicking at the sand. SUNNY spins a small flower between her paws, humming softly. HOWEY walks calmly beside LUNA and AMIE. MISTY drifts quietly at the back. Her Moonstone flickering faintly. Something pulling at her. She doesn!t know what yet. ZENNY floats just above the sand, eyes half closed.  9  FUZZBY Suddenly! A giant shadow loomed over the  Brave Bee! Was it a monster? Was it a mountain?  10  KEEN (stepping over Fuzzby)  It was my left foot, Fuzzby. Watch out.  11 FUZZBY (undeterred)  Our hero narrowly escaped  being squished into a pancake!  Misty slows. Her Moonstone pulses. She looks toward the rocks.  12  MISTY (quietly, almost to herself) Something feels… different.  Zenny drifts over to Misty.  13  ZENNY What are you feeling?  14  MISTY (concentrating) Something small.  Something frightened. It!s close.  Zenny looks slowly along the beach. Then — quietly — raises one small paw. Points. Near the water!s edge — almost hidden in the shadow of a rock — A small FOX CUB. Misty looks where Zenny is pointing. Her Moonstone pulses strongly.  15  MISTY (softly) That!s it. 
 
 
That!s what I felt.  The group slows instinctively. Nobody speaks. Nobody moves too fast. They approach gently, spreading into a loose wide circle. The cub doesn!t move. Just watches. Shaking slightly. Its fur is unkempt. Its eyes are huge and wary. Misty!s Moonstone pulses again. Stronger this time. She was right. Something IS different. Sunny!s heart melts immediately. She kneels first.  16  SUNNY We should call her Blossom!  Everyone smiles. Howey kneels slowly and carefully. Big warm smile.  17  HOWEY Hi. We are the Crystal Bears. We are very glad we found you.  The cub defensively growls softly.  Luna gently touches Howey!s arm. He looks at her. Nods. Stands back up quietly. Luna steps forward. Kneels at Blossom!s level. Her voice barely above a whisper.  18  LUNA You are safe here. We are not going anywhere. Take all the time you need.  The cub!s ears flick. It takes one tiny step forward. Then stops. Fuzzby sees this as progress. He reaches into his satchel and produces a small honey dip with a flourish.  19 FUZZBY (grandly) Allow me.  The cub SNATCHES it — then leaps backwards defensively. Growls softly. Fuzzby jumps back in shock — CRASHES into Keen. Keen stumbles backwards — ONTO HOWEY.  
 
 
20  KEEN (in Howey!s arms) This is not what it looks like.  21  HOWEY It looks like you need a cuddle.  22  KEEN I really do.  The cub watches all of this with enormous suspicious eyes. Chewing the honey dip very fast in case someone takes it back. Fuzzby carefully places another honey dip on the sand and slides it forward.  23  FUZZBY (grandly) Behold! The Golden Goo of Friendship!  The cub snatches it instantly — then retreats again.  24 FUZZBY (quietly, to Keen) I was being nice.  25  KEEN (also quietly) I know.  The group pauses. Looking at each other. Not sure what to do next.  26  KEEN Maybe… she doesn!t want help?  27  AMIE Or maybe she doesn!t trust us yet.  MISTY drifts forward very slowly. She doesn!t reach out. She just settles nearby. Closes her eyes. A breath. Her Moonstone glows softly.  28  MISTY Sometimes… stillness helps.  
 
 
ZENNY drifts forward too. Even slower than Misty. She settles right beside the cub. Doesn!t look at it. Doesn!t reach out. Just closes her eyes. Breathes. The cub watches her. Its trembling slows. Just slightly. Just enough. The others follow their lead. Nobody moves. Nobody speaks. The cub watches. Still unsure. But it stops growling. Misty opens her eyes slowly. Her Moonstone pulses with a soft pearl light. She looks at the paw prints in the sand around the cub. Going in circles. Back and forth. Everywhere.  29 MISTY (quietly) She!s not just scared. She!s lost.  She!s been trying to find her way back.  Everyone looks at the prints.  30  KEEN They go everywhere…  31 MISTY My crystal… I can feel something. Like a thread. I think I can follow it.  Misty holds her Moonstone gently. It glows warmer. Pulling in one direction. She looks up toward the rocks at the end of the beach.  32 MISTY That way.  33  FUZZBY (excited) I love this! It!s like a map! Maps are like puzzles you can walk on!  The cub looks at Misty. Then at the direction she pointed. Then back at Misty. Something in the cub!s eyes shifts. Just slightly. Just enough. She takes one small step forward. Then another. She is coming with them. Misty turns quietly toward the treeline at the edge of the beach. Her Moonstone pulls her gently. Not toward the rocks yet. Toward something else first.  CUT TO: 
 
 
 EXT. CRYSTAL COVE – CRYSTAL HOLLOW                    4  At the edge of the beach where the sand meets the rocks — A small hidden hollow. Surrounded by a cluster of natural crystals growing from the ground. Glinting softly in the light. Inside — carefully arranged: Berries. Flowers. Pieces of honeycomb. A small, neat food stash. Someone has been collecting for a while. Surviving. Alone. But she chose this spot. Because even lost — she was drawn to the crystals. The group stops. Takes it in. Nobody speaks.   34  MISTY (very softly) She!s been here… a long time.  Blossom looks at her stash. Then at the bears. Something in her face changes. She has been holding this in for a very long time. Her bottom lip trembles.  35  BLOSSOM (small, scared) I… I want my Mummy.  A beat. The words hang in the air. Zenny drifts in quietly. She settles beside Blossom. Gentle. Steady.   Luna steps forward softly.  36 LUNA You are safe here. We will help you find her.  From behind a nearby tree — FUZZBY has somehow ended up sitting on top of HOWEY!S HEAD. Crying his little eyes out. Howey!s big fluffy hair is absolutely soaking. Dripping. Howey stands completely still beneath him. Not bothered in the slightest.  36  FUZZBY (sobbing) She’s just a baby Howey. A TINY baby.  37  HOWEY 
 
 
I know.  38  FUZZBY With tiny little paws.  39  HOWEY I know Fuzzby.  40  FUZZBY And she just wanted her Mummy!  41  HOWEY I know.  Beat. A drop of Fuzzby!s tears drips slowly off the end of Howey!s nose. Howey watches it fall.  42  HOWEY I am getting wet now Fuzzby.  43  FUZZBY I know.  44  FUZZBY Your fur can HANDLE it!  ZENNY drifts over quietly. She reaches into her sack. Pulls out a small soft leaf. Holds it up to Fuzzby. Fuzzby takes it. Dabs his eyes very delicately. Then blows his nose. HONK. An enormous sound for such a tiny bee. Everyone looks. Fuzzby looks at the soggy leaf. Then holds it out to Zenny.  45  FUZZBY Thank you Zenny. You can have it back now.  Zenny stares at the soggy leaf.  46  ZENNY I do not want that slimy thing back.  Fuzzby looks at the leaf.  47  FUZZBY Fair.  
 
 
They both laugh. Everyone laughs. Even Blossom. Just a tiny one. But it is real.  Misty looks at Blossom. Her Moonstone glows warm. She reaches out her paw. Blossom looks at it. Then slowly… places her tiny paw in Misty!s.  48  MISTY (softly) Let!s find her.  CUT TO:  EXT. SLIPPERY ROCKS – DAY                             5 The soft sand gives way to jagged wet rocks and tangled seaweed. The group picks their way carefully across. Blossom hops between rocks surprisingly nimbly. But the trail has gone cold. No paw prints on the rock. Nothing to follow. Blossom looks around desperately. Misty watches her. Her Moonstone pulses. She steps forward. Places both paws over her crystal.  49  MISTY Feeling deep and true, seeing old and new — Moonstone, help us through.  Pearl light blooms from her crystal. Soft and warm. It spreads slowly across the wet rocks. Then — faintly — tiny paw prints appear glowing soft pearl white. Leading through the rocks toward a gap in the boulders. Misty opens her eyes.  50  MISTY (CONT!D) That way. She came through here.  Then — a LARGE RED CRAB skitters out from a dark crevice. It plants itself directly in their path. Hoisting two oversized jagged claws into the air. CLACK! CLACK-CLACK!  51  FUZZBY (diving behind Keen) AHH! A rock with scissors! It!s come to give us a haircut!  
 
 
52  KEEN (sighs) It!s just a crab Fuzzby. He!s probably just guarding his porch.  The crab doesn!t move. Claws waving.  53  SUNNY He looks a little grumpy. Maybe he hasn!t had his snack today?  54  FUZZBY (peeking out) A snack?! I have a honey dip! Do crabs like honey?  55  HOWEY No honey Fuzzby.  The crab raises its right claw. Gives a slow deliberate CLACK. Staring right at Howey!s feet. Fuzzby flies down. Lands on a nearby piece of kelp. He begins to scuttle back and forth. Doing a sideways shimmy. Making tiny snapping motions with his bee legs.  56  FUZZBY (high-pitched clicky voice) Click-buzz-clack! Snip-snap-whirr!  The crab stops clacking. Its stalky eyes swivel toward Fuzzby.  57  FUZZBY (whispering to the bears) I think I accidentally told him his shell looks very shiny today.  The crab looks at Fuzzby. Then at Blossom. Then — points one sharp claw toward a gap in the boulders. Right where the rocks open up.  58  KEEN (blinking) Did he just… give us directions?  59  FUZZBY (smugly) 
 
 
I told you Keen. I am a very talented bee.  The crab gives one final CLACK and scuttles back into his hole.  60  ZENNY (deadpan) I am not going to ask how you knew that.  61  FUZZBY (smugly) Wise.  Fuzzby looks at the gap in the boulders. The exact gap the crab pointed to. The exact gap Misty!s pearl paw prints were already leading to. Misty looks at the gap. Her crystal already glowing toward it. She smiles quietly. Says nothing.  62  MISTY (quietly) Through here. She!s close.  CUT TO:  EXT. FOREST EDGE – CRYSTAL COVE                       6 The rocks give way to a soft mossy opening where the beach meets the forest. Warm light filters through the trees. The sound of the sea behind them. The forest ahead. Misty!s Moonstone is glowing its brightest yet. She walks forward slowly. Following the feeling. The thread pulling her toward the treeline.  63  MISTY (very softly) She!s there. I can feel it.  Howey places a gentle paw on Blossom!s shoulder.  64  HOWEY (quietly) You go first.  
 
 
Blossom looks at the treeline. Takes a deep breath. Lets out a long clear YIP-YIP-YIP.  From between the trees — a MUMMY FOX rushes out. Looking frantic. Eyes searching. She sees Blossom. Blossom BOLTS straight to her. They crash together in the soft moss. Nuzzling. Pressing close. The mother fox wraps herself around Blossom completely. Holding on. The bears watch in silence. Sunny!s chin wobbles. Amie grabs Keen!s arm. Howey puts a paw over his heart. Fuzzby makes absolutely no sound whatsoever. Which means he is crying the hardest. Blossom looks back at the bears.  65  BLOSSOM (small, grateful) Thank you.  She disappears into the trees with her mum. But then — The mother fox steps back out. Just for a moment. She looks at the bears. Holds their gaze. A long quiet moment. Then she dips her head slowly. A quiet thank you that needs no words. The bears dip their heads back. She turns and disappears into the forest. Gone.  The forest edge settles. The bears stand quietly. A long peaceful moment.  KEEN is very still. He looks down at his wristbands. Turns them slowly. Watching Blossom disappear with her Mum makes something stir in him. A child finding her way back to the person she loves most. He thinks of his own Mum. Back on the pier. The day she placed these wristbands in his hands. He doesn!t say anything. He doesn!t need to.  MISTY settles quietly beside him. She doesn!t speak. Just sits. Close enough that he knows she!s there.  66  KEEN (quietly) She found her way home.  67  MISTY They always do.  
 
 
Her Moonstone glows soft and warm. The forest is quiet. Everything is exactly as it should be.  CUT TO:  EXT. CRYSTAL COVE BEACH – SUNSET                      7 The sky burns gold and pink. Waves lap gently at the shore. The bears sit together on the sand. Easy. Quiet. Happy. Fuzzby has recovered. Mostly. He creeps toward Keen!s satchel. Very sneaky. Very slow. One tiny step at a time. He reaches it. Begins to ease the strap open. A sound behind him. He freezes. Turns slowly. The fox cub has reappeared from the trees. She watches Fuzzby with enormous knowing eyes. Fuzzby stares at her. She stares back. Then she TACKLES him. Licking his face. Wings everywhere. Fuzzby flails — laughing despite himself.  68  FUZZBY (laughing, flailing) Hey — hey! I was being sneaky!  The honeycomb tumbles out of the satchel. Lands on the sand. The cub sees it. Grabs it. Tiny legs. GONE. Back into the trees. Fuzzby sits up. Sandy. Defeated. Slightly damp from being licked. He watches the trees where she disappeared. Keen sits down beside him.  69  KEEN …worth it?  Fuzzby thinks about it. Really thinks about it.  70  FUZZBY …worth it.  Beat. Zenny drifts over. Settles nearby. A small peaceful smile.  71  ZENNY Always.  They sit together in the golden light. The waves lap gently. The forest is quiet. Everything is exactly as it should be.  FADE OUT.
