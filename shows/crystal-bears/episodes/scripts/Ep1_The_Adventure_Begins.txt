FADE IN:

EXT. DEEP WITHIN THE RAINFOREST – DAY				1				                         

Tall flowers sway gently in the warm breeze. Some stretch high above the grass, with long soft necks and bright open petals full of pollen. FUZZBY and ZENNY weave between them collecting pollen from flower to flower. Tiny pollen sacks bounce on their back legs. Zenny works with smooth calm precision — neat, efficient, graceful. Fuzzby does not. He zig-zags wildly through the air, humming loudly to himself as he works.

FUZZBY
(fun sing-song rhythm)
BIZZY-BIZZY-BIZZY, BIZZY-BIZZY-BIZZY…

He dips low into a flower, scoops up pollen — then overdoes it and spins sideways into a leaf. FWIP. He bounces back into the air and straightens up proudly.

	2	 FUZZBY (CONT’D)
Nailed it.

Zenny glides up beside him. Watches him for a beat.


He zooms to another flower, humming louder, and sticks his whole face into it. A beat. He pulls back out completely dusted in pollen like a fuzzy yellow moustache. Zenny tries not to laugh.

7	 FUZZBY (CONT’D)
Do I look official?

8 	ZENNY
Yes Fuzzby
Officially nuts! 

Fuzzby gasps. He wipes at his face. Somehow smears the pollen worse. He dives again toward another flower — clips a branch — tumbles end over end through the air. THUP. THUP. THUP. He lands upside down inside a blossom. Beat. His little legs kick. Then he pops back up.

9	 FUZZBY
(popping up)
Buzz Crash!!

Zenny rolls her eyes, but she is smiling now.
Fuzzby hums louder and dives into another flower.
He pops back out, somehow even more covered in pollen than before.

Zenny watches him. Beat. A distant RUMBLE of thunder.
Fuzzby pauses mid-hover.

10 	FUZZBY (CONT’D)
…okay that sounded dramatic.

Zenny glances toward the sky. The light has shifted slightly. Something feels different. But they do not see anything yet.

11	 ZENNY
A Storm’s coming.

12	 FUZZBY
Good thing I work well under pressure.

He immediately flies into a flower and gets stuck again. Zenny sighs.

CUT TO:

EXT. CRYSTAL COVE – AIDA’S SANCTUARY – DAY				2		 		                    

Gentle waves roll onto the shore. AIDA sits in stillness beside a crystal bowl. She runs the wand softly around the rim. A low shimmering tone fills the air. The cove seems to quiet around her.
She closes her eyes. Her ROSE QUARTZ PENDANT begins to glow. Soft pink light rises across her face.

AIDA’S INNER VISION — THROUGH AIDA’S EYES - A wooden pier. A small sailboat waiting on bright blue water. KEEN standing beside his MUM. He checks the straps on his satchel. Then checks them again.
Then one more time. His Mum watches him with a proud smile that cannot quite hide her worry.

BACK TO AIDA

The pink glow fades gently from her eyes. Aida places a paw over her heart. A soft knowing smile.

13 	AIDA
(softly)
It’s time.

She rises gracefully. The bowl’s note fades into the sound of distant wind. Aida turns and heads purposefully into the forest.

CUT TO:

EXT. KEEN’S ISLAND – THE PIER – DAY				 	3                             

A neat little sailboat bobs beside the dock. KEEN loads his final supplies onto the boat — a rolled map, a satchel, a berry pouch, a blanket. He arranges them carefully. Then rearranges them. Then checks them one more time. His MUM stands nearby, smiling proudly, though her eyes are glassy.

14 	KEEN’S MUM
Keen… are you sure you’ve got everything?

15 	KEEN
I’ve got this, Mum.

He pats the satchel. Pauses. Checks one more pocket just in case. His Mum smiles despite herself.

16 	KEEN’S MUM
I know… it’s just…
this is a big step.

Keen looks out at the water. He swallows. He is trying very hard to be brave.

17 	KEEN
Like you said…
it’s part of growing up.

His Mum nods softly. She reaches into her bag and takes out two worn WRISTBANDS. She holds them carefully, like they matter. Keen sees them and stills.

18	 KEEN’S MUM
Keen…
it’s time for you to have your father’s wristbands.

A small breeze moves through the ropes along the pier. Keen looks down at them. His expression softens.

19	 KEEN’S MUM (CONT’D)
I still feel him…
every day.

She places the wristbands gently into Keen’s paws. He turns them over carefully. Feels their weight. Their meaning.

20 	KEEN’S MUM (CONT’D)
Promise me you’ll always wear them.

21 	KEEN
(quietly/proud)
I promise.

He slips them onto his wrists. They fit perfectly. He turns his paws, taking them in. His Mum brushes a paw gently against his cheek.

22	 KEEN’S MUM
Wherever you go…
You’ll never be alone.

Keen takes that in. A small, real smile.

23 	KEEN
I won’t be.
(softly)
I love you, Mum.

24 	KEEN’S MUM
I love you…
my brave boy.

Keen climbs into the boat. He unties the rope. Pushes off. The boat drifts gently away from the dock. His Mum lifts a paw and waves. Keen waves back. Trying to look confident. Then the boat wobbles unexpectedly. He flails. Catches himself. Pretends that was meant to happen.

25	KEEN
Still got it!

His Mum laughs through a tear. Suddenly — SPLASH! A DOLPHIN rises beside the boat. Keen startles. Then lights up.

26	 KEEN (CONT’D)
Whoa!

The Dolphin chirps happily and leaps again through the sparkling water. Keen leans over the side, delighted.

27 	KEEN (CONT’D)
Hey there!

The Dolphin chatters. Keen nods as though he fully understands.

28	 KEEN (CONT’D)
Nice to meet you too.

The boat glides farther from the dock. His Mum grows smaller behind him. Keen looks ahead. A little nervous. A little excited. A lot of both.

CUT TO:

EXT. AT SEA – DAY   				 			4                                           	

Open water. Endless blue. Keen sails confidently — or at least he tries to. The friendly DOLPHIN keeps pace beside the boat, popping up every so often.

29 	KEEN
Hi! I’m Keen!
Nice to meet you, Squeaky!

The Dolphin chatters happily. Keen nods like that settles it.

30	 KEEN (CONT’D)
Squeaky. Perfect.
You really do sound squeaky.

Squeaky leaps high beside the boat. Keen laughs. He looks at his map. Then ahead. Then back at his map. A little uncertain.

31	 KEEN (CONT’D)
Okay…
so if this is north…
then I am definitely going…

He turns the map upside down.

32 	KEEN (CONT’D)
…one of the directions.

A gust of wind hits. The sail FLAPS hard. The boat lurches. Keen stumbles sideways and grabs the mast.

33	 KEEN (CONT’D)
Oh!

The sky darkens. Clouds roll in faster than he expected. Squeaky disappears beneath the water. Another gust. Stronger. Rain starts as a few drops. Then many. Then sheets. The sea roughens. The boat CREAKS. Keen tries to keep hold of the map while steering. The wind SNATCHES it from his paws.

34	 KEEN (CONT’D)
No no no no—!

He lunges for it. Misses. The map whips through the air and vanishes into the storm. Keen stands frozen. Rainwater sloshes around his feet. The horizon disappears behind sheets of grey.

35	 KEEN (CONT’D)
(under his breath)
Oh no…
I’m lost!!!

Lightning flickers far away. Keen grips the edge of the boat tighter. His breathing quickens. He closes his eyes for just a second. Breathes. Grounds himself.

36	 KEEN (CONT’D)
(quiet, steadying)
I’ve got this…
I’ve got this…

A familiar CLICKING CHATTER sounds nearby. Keen opens his eyes. Squeaky leaps through the storm water beside the boat. He dips and reappears, signaling insistently in one direction.

37 	KEEN (CONT’D)
Follow you?

Squeaky chirps. Keen looks at the rough water. Then at Squeaky. Then nods.

38	 KEEN (CONT’D)
Okay…
lead on, Squeaky!

Squeaky surges ahead. Keen wrestles the little boat around to follow.

CUT TO:

EXT. RAINFOREST EDGE – CONTINUOUS					5                               

The wind picks up around the trees. Leaves shiver. Branches sway harder now. Fuzzby and Zenny move quickly through the air, heading back through the forest. Fuzzby flies slightly ahead — still talking, because of course he is.

39	 FUZZBY
I’m just saying, if this turns into a full storm situation, 
I am extremely—

He stops dead in mid-air. Completely still. Zenny nearly bumps into him.

40 	ZENNY
Fuzzby?

No response. Fuzzby slowly raises one tiny paw… Points. Zenny follows his gaze. Out beyond the trees. Through the shifting wind and rain, a tiny boat rises and falls in the distance. Barely visible in the storm. Beat. Fuzzby’s eyes go WIDE.

41 	FUZZBY
…that is NOT a good place to be.

Zenny’s expression softens immediately.

42 	ZENNY
Someone’s in trouble.

Fuzzby snaps into action.

43	 FUZZBY
BEARS! BEARS! EMERGENCY BEARS!

He BLASTS off toward the beach at full speed. Immediately flies into a branch. BONK. He recoils. Shakes it off. Keeps going anyway. Zenny follows behind — calm, focused. This time faster.

CUT TO:

EXT. CRYSTAL COVE – STORM BUILDING  				6                            	
Dark clouds gather over the cove. The Crystal Bears stand together on the shore. AIDA at the front. HOWEY, MISTY, LUNA and the others nearby. They look out at the sea as the wind moves through the grasses. Fuzzby comes HURTLING in. Too fast. He tries to stop. Fail. He pinballs off a shell, ricochets past Howey, loops around a piece of driftwood and lands in a soggy heap. Beat. One tiny paw lifts.

44 	FUZZBY
I meant to do that.

Nobody reacts. He scrambles up, wild-eyed.

45	 FUZZBY (CONT’D)
BEARS! BEARS! There’s someone out there
and they’re —
they’re — they’re —

He bends over, completely out of breath. Cannot get the words out. Zenny floats in behind him, steady as ever.

46	 ZENNY
In trouble.

Fuzzby points at her gratefully.

47 	FUZZBY
IN TROUBLE!
YES! THAT!

Aida steps forward to the water’s edge. The wind stirs her fur gently, but she remains still. She looks out across the stormy sea. Through the rain we glimpse a tiny boat rising and falling between waves.

48	 AIDA
There he is.

The bears all look out. Concern. Hope. 
Aida turns to them.

49	 AIDA (CONT’D)
Everyone —
activate your crystal powers.

The bears gather closer.

CRYSTAL CALL SEQUENCE.

Aida touches her pendant.

50 	AIDA (CONT’D)
With open heart and love so bright —
Rose Quartz Crystal, shine your light!

Her ROSE QUARTZ glows warmly. The others touch their crystals one by one. A pulse of color from each. Misty’s crystal glows. Luna’s. Howey’s. Zenny’s. Together the bears speak as one.

51	 ALL
With love and with light, together unite —
Crystal Bears, shine your light!

A brilliant BURST OF COMBINED LIGHT shoots out across the storm-dark sea. It cuts through the rain like a beacon.

CUT TO:

EXT. OUT AT SEA – STORM	  						7                                       

The beam of crystal light reaches through the grey.
Keen sees it.
His face lifts with hope.

52	 KEEN
What is that…?

Then — Squeaky cries out. Keen looks down sharply. The Dolphin is struggling. A ragged drift net is wrapped tightly around his tail, dragging him under.

53 	KEEN (CONT’D)
Squeaky!

The boat bucks hard beneath Keen. He nearly goes overboard. Rain lashes his face. He looks at the storm. Looks at Squeaky. No hesitation.

54	 KEEN (CONT’D)
Hang on!

He dives into the water.

SHORE – INTERCUT

The Crystal Bears watch tensely. Fuzzby’s mouth hangs open.

55 	HOWIE
He jumped in?!

56	 AIDA
(softly, impressed)
He didn’t think twice.

UNDERWATER

Everything goes muffled and blue-grey. Keen swims hard through swirling water toward Squeaky. He reaches him. The drift net is twisted tightly around the dolphin’s tail. Pulling him down. Keen grabs the net and pulls. Nothing. The current shoves them sideways. Keen slips. Reaches again.

57 	KEEN
(muffled)
I’ve got you!

Squeaky thrashes weakly. Keen pulls again. Still nothing. His eyes flick upward toward the churning light above. He is running out of strength. A beat. Keen sees his father’s wristbands on his arms. A faint glow begins inside them. Soft. Steady. He plants his feet against a submerged rock. Centers himself.

58	 KEEN (CONT’D)
(to himself)
I’ve got this…
I’ve got this…


SHORE – INTERCUT

Aida watches with calm certainty.

59	 AIDA
(gently)
He’s not alone.

UNDERWATER

Keen grips the net with both paws. The wristbands glow brighter. He PULLS. The net strains. Still tangled. Above them, the shape of a MASSIVE WAVE gathers. Keen looks at Squeaky.

60 	KEEN
Come on…

He braces again.

61 	KEEN (CONT’D)
(stronger)
COME ON!

He yanks with everything he has. SNAP! The net TEARS. In the same instant the massive wave crashes overhead. The world explodes into foam and motion. Keen is thrown sideways. He tumbles through bubbles and dark water. Disoriented. Then he sees Squeaky.

62	 KEEN (CONT’D)
Stay with me!

Squeaky surges up beneath him. Keen grabs hold. The dolphin rockets toward the surface. They BURST out of the water. Keen gasps, coughs, then laughs from pure relief.

63	 KEEN (CONT’D)
You got me!

Squeaky chirps. Keen clings on as the dolphin races through the storm toward the glowing shore.

SHORE POV

The Crystal Bears watch as a shape rises over the waves. It is Keen — riding Squeaky in on the crest. Howey’s jaw drops. Misty claps a paw over her mouth.

64 	SUNNY
The bravest guy I’ve ever seen.

65 	MISTY
His boat’s gone!!

Then, as Keen comes closer, Fuzzby’s panic flips instantly into awe.

66	 FUZZBY (CONT’D)
(awestruck)
When I grow up…
I wanna be like him.

Keen bounces over a wave like a tiny cowboy. One paw in the air. A huge grin across his face.

67 KEEN
YEEHAW!!

Despite the storm, everyone smiles.

CUT TO:

EXT. CRYSTAL COVE BEACH – DAY	    					8                               

Squeaky glides into the shallows. Keen hops off onto the sand, stumbling forward. Laughing. Breathless. Soaked from head to toe.

68 	KEEN
Best… ride… ever!

His legs wobble. He nearly falls. Howey instinctively steps in and steadies him. The bears gather around him in a warm circle. SUNNY bounces excitedly. AMIE beams. Everyone curious, gentle, smiling. Keen looks around at them all.

69 	KEEN
Hi… I’m Keen.
Fuzzby zooms right into Keen’s face.

70	 FUZZBY
You rode a dolphin.
Into a storm.
On purpose?!

Keen blinks at him.

71	 KEEN
…kind of?

Fuzzby slowly turns to the others. Points at Keen like he has discovered a legend.

72 	FUZZBY
He’s amazing.

Zenny gently drifts in and pulls Fuzzby back by one leg.

73 	ZENNY
Give him space.

The others smile. Together:

74	 ALL
Welcome to Crystal Cove!
We’re the Crystal Bears!

Keen looks around at the cove — glowing crystals, warm faces, the gentle shelter of the beach. He takes it all in.

75	 KEEN
Wow… this place…

He turns back toward the sea. Squeaky leaps once more just beyond the shallows. Aida steps forward beside Keen. She lifts a paw and makes a small, gentle gesture to the dolphin. Squeaky watches her. Seems to understand. He dips his head once. Then dives back into the waves. Keen watches him go.

76	 KEEN (CONT’D)
What did you do?
Aida smiles.

77 	AIDA
Just getting a message home to your Mum.
To let her know you’re okay.

Keen absorbs that. A soft grateful pause.

78 	KEEN
Thank you.

Aida gives him the smallest nod.

EXT. GATHERING AREA – DAY						9	                                       

The storm has passed. Everything glows fresh and washed clean. Droplets cling to leaves and sparkle in the sunlight. The Crystal Bears gather in a quiet circle. Aida and Keen stand at the center. Keen still wears his father’s wristbands. Aida looks at him with warmth and certainty.

79	 AIDA
You followed your heart…
and chose courage.

A gentle AQUAMARINE LIGHT begins to shimmer in the air between them. A glowing crystal appears. Keen stares in wonder. The crystal lifts, humming softly, then splits into two smaller gems. They float toward his wrists. One settles into each wristband. Perfectly. A pulse of AZURE LIGHT runs through them. Keen feels it instantly.
He lifts his paws, amazed. The light reflects in his eyes.

80 	KEEN
(with rising confidence)
Brave and bright, with all my might —
Aquamarine Crystal, shine with light!

His wristbands glow brighter. The energy settles warmly around him. Fuzzby claps too early. Stops. Then starts clapping again louder to cover it. Howey cheers. Misty beams. Luna smiles softly. And suddenly the whole group surges in and SWAMPS Keen in a giant bear hug. Keen startles. Then melts into it. He hugs them back. 
The group slowly breaks apart. Keen looks around at all of them. No longer lost. No longer alone.

81 	AIDA
That’s what family does.
(ALT)
Wherever you go…
You’ll never be alone.


Keen smiles. A full, real, homecoming smile.

CUT TO:

EXT. CRYSTAL COVE BEACH – CONTINUOUS				10	                      

The mood has lifted. Waves lap gently at the shore. Keen and the bears walk together along the sand. Fuzzby buzzes up beside Keen, suddenly very serious.

82	 FUZZBY
So…
quick question.

83	 KEEN
Yeah?

84	 FUZZBY
How do you feel about honey?

Keen looks at him, still catching his breath from everything.

85	 KEEN
Uh…
I like it?

Beat. Fuzzby GASPS. His eyes sparkle. This is the best news he has heard all day.

86	 FUZZBY
Oh… this is going to work out VERY well.

87 	ZENNY
Fuzzby…

But Fuzzby is already buzzing excitedly around Keen.

88 	FUZZBY
Honey cakes, honey snacks, honey naps,
honey leaf drizzles, emergency honey—

Zenny gently catches one of his legs and pulls him back.

89 	ZENNY
One thing at a time.

90 	FUZZBY
I have waited my whole life for this friendship!

Keen laughs. The others laugh too as they continue walking together down the beach. The camera lingers on the cove, bright and peaceful now.

FADE OUT.

THE END
