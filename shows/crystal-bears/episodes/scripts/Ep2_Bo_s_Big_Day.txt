FADE IN: EXT. BUZZING NOOK – MORNING 1  Golden morning light. Dew sparkles on wildflowers. A honeycomb sits on a small stump. KEEN tiptoes toward it. He lifts his knees high with every step.  CRUNCH. CRUNCH. CRUNCH.  Keen freezes each time. He spots the honeycomb. His eyes widen. He LICKS HIS LIPS. He reaches toward the honey. FUZZBY the bee is asleep on top of the honeycomb. FUZZBY (snoring) 1 ZZZZZ …  A small snore bubble grows from his nose. Keen reaches closer to the honey. POP! The bubble bursts. Keen FREEZES. FUZZBY (snoring,again) 2 ZZZZZ …  Another bubble slowly grows. Keen inches closer to the honey.  POP!The second bubble bursts.  A tiny puff of yellow pollen floats through the air… … and lands right on Keen's nose.  Beat.  Keen's nose TWITCHES. His eyes water. He tries to hold it. He holds it. He holds it. He almost stops — 
 KEEN (Sneeze) 3 ACHOO!  Fuzzby's eyes POP open. Beat. FUZZBY 4 Hi Keen.  Fuzzby's eyes close again. Instant SNORING. FUZZBY (snoring,again) 5 ZZZZZ …  Keen sighs and walks away. Nearby, ZENNY sits cross-legged on a leaf. Eyes closed. ZENNY 6 Ommmm…  Fuzzby snores louder. CUT TO: EXT. CRYSTAL CLEARING – MORNING 2  Soft mist floats through tall trees. AIDA sits beside a crystal bowl. She slowly circles a mallet around the rim. A warm tone hums through the clearing. Her ROSE QUARTZ PENDANT begins to glow. Pink light fills her eyes.  VISION – THROUGH AIDA'S EYES  A small red squirrel sits inside a hollow tree. He packs a satchel. Unpacks it. Packs it again. He doesn't move. BACK TO SCENE. Aida gently stops the bowl. She places a paw on her heart.  
AIDA (softly) 7 Someone needs a little help today.  Across the clearing Keen brushes pollen from his nose. AIDA 8 Keen? Come with me.  Keen jogs over. KEEN 9 Coming, Aida!  CUT TO: INT. BO'S HOLLOW OAK – DAY 3  Warm sunlight fills a cozy hollow tree. BO, a young red squirrel, sits beside a small satchel. Next to him lies a shiny CONKER. BO (to the conker) 10 I am going to go.  In a minute. Or forty.  He places the conker in the bag. Then takes it out. Then puts it back again. A shadow appears at the doorway. Keen gently taps on the hollow tree. TAP TAP. He peeks inside.  Beat. KEEN 11 Hi! I'm Keen.  Aida sent me.  She thought we should meet.  Bo jumps. The conker flies into the air. He catches it and hugs it. 
BO 12 What if nobody likes me at the Learning Circle? What if my tail does the THING?  His tail suddenly POOFS twice its size. Bo grabs it. BO 13 Stop it!  The tail refuses. POOFS twice its size again.  Keen stares. Impressed. KEEN 14 Whoa.  Your tail is AMAZING. BO 15 …you think so? KEEN 16 Mine does nothing.  Watch. Keen turns around and wiggles. His tail hangs there. Completely still. KEEN 17 See?  Nothing.  Bo looks at Keen's tail. Then at his own. His tail slowly deflates. He almost smiles. CUT TO: EXT. FOREST PATH – DAY 4  A winding path through the Whispering Woods. Sunlight flickers through the leaves. In the distance we hear happy voices from the LEARNING CIRCLE. Bo walks beside Keen. Step. Step. Then Bo suddenly stops. He grips his satchel tightly. 
BO (quiet) 18 I don't think I can go to the Learning Circle.  Keen gently sits on a fallen log beside him. He gives Bo space. Bo stares down the path. BO 19 They all know each other.  Beat. BO 20 I don’t know them, and they don't know me.  Leaves rustle above. AIDA climbs down from a small ridge beside the path, brushing twigs from her fur. A leaf is stuck behind her ear. AIDA 21 Tricky trees. Bo glances up. Keen smiles. KEEN 22 Bo, this is Aida.  Aida sits beside Bo, leaving space between them. She notices his satchel. AIDA 23 You packed your bag. BO 24 Four times. AIDA (warm) 25 I would have done five.  Bo looks at her. 
BO 26 You get scared too? AIDA 27 Every single time. BO 28 But you still go? AIDA 29 Every single time. BEAT. AIDA 30 Every single time.  Wind moves gently through the trees. CUT TO: EXT. FOREST PATH – CONTINUOUS 5  Aida offers her paw. Bo hesitates. Keen gives a small nod. Aida touches her pendant. AIDA 31 With heart open wide, I stand with pride — Rose Quartz, be our guide!  Pink light spreads across the forest floor. Keen touches his wristbands. KEEN 32 Brave and bright, with all my might — Aquamarine, shine with light!  Blue light joins the pink. The forest path glows. Bo takes a step. Then another. They walk together. CUT TO: 
EXT. LEARNING CIRCLE – DAY 6  The other bears sit together in a sunny clearing. SUNNY jumps up excitedly, practically bouncing. SUNNY 33 HI! I'm Sunny! Do you like cake? I like singing and dancing … and cake!  Sunny spins happily in a quick little twirl. LUNA 34 Sunny. Breathe.  Sunny takes a huge breath. SUNNY 35 Right. Breathing.  Bo carefully sits on a cushion. His tail suddenly POOFS huge. Everyone sees. Bo freezes.  Beat. MISTY (delighted) 36 Can it do that again?  The tail POOFS again. Bigger. Everyone cheers. AMIE 37 Do it again!  KIT giggles as the fluffy tail tickles his nose. Bo laughs. His tail popping like a fluffy firework. CUT TO: EXT. LEARNING CIRCLE – LATER 7  Afternoon sunlight fills the clearing. Bo draws pictures of the bears on a large leaf. Keen sits beside him. 
BO 38 That one is you. Look at your tail.  Keen lifts the drawing. His tail in the picture is enormous. KEEN 39 My tail is doing a THING! They laugh. BO (quiet) 40 I'm glad I came to the learning circle – even though I was scared to at first. KEEN 41 Me too.  Bo’s tail suddenly POOFS twice its size. Bo and Keen giggle. Aida watches from the edge of the clearing. Her pendant glows softly. She smiles. CUT TO: EXT. KEEN'S GARDEN – EVENING 8  Keen tends to his garden. Golden sunset. The honeycomb still sits untouched.  FUZZBY sneaks through the grass wearing a FLOWER DISGUISE — petals around his head like he's trying to blend in. He freezes, pretending to be a flower. Nothing happens. He peeks around. Creeps toward the honey. A twig SNAPs.  He freezes. Moves again … Trips into a BUZZ-CRASH. He face-plants into Keen's back. Keen does not turn around. He simply raises one paw. KEEN 42 Hi Fuzzby.  Bye Fuzzby. 
Fuzzby gives a thumbs-up from the grass. Nearby, ZENNY sits cross-legged on her leaf. ZENNY 43 Ommmm…  She slowly opens her eyes. She sees Fuzzby tangled in the flower petals. Zenny smiles. She giggles, sighs, and then closes her eyes and returns to meditation. ZENNY 44 Ommmm… WIDE SHOT — Keen's peaceful garden in golden light. Fuzzby stuck in the petals. The honeycomb remains untouched. FADE OUT.  END.
