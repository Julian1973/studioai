She steps back with a satisfied smile.  10  SUNNY Cups straight, gorgeous garland… and the weather is perfect. Everything is exactly where it needs to be. Nothing is going to ruin this party.  Plink. 12  SUNNY (slight panic) It!s fine! It!s okay! Everything is fine!  Then the sky opens up. Rain pours over the decorations. Garlands droop. Lanterns hang wet. Berry cups fill with rainwater. PARTY CAVE – CONTINUOUS                          3  Sunny bursts inside, completely soaked, her arms overflowing with soggy, tangled decorations. The other bears immediately jump into action. HOWEY grabs the wet lanterns. MISTY tries to untangle a massive knot of ribbons. LUNA attempts to dry off the flooded berry cups.  15  SUNNY (spinning around) Lanterns over here! Ribbons over there! Hurry, before the pretty paper gets muddy!  Keen walks over, holding out a warm, dry towel.  16  KEEN Sunny, it!s okay. We are safe inside now. Let!s just dry off.  Sunny shakes her head fast, her wet ears flopping.   
 
 
17  SUNNY No time for drying! We must make the inside look exactly like the outside! Keen, bring the ladder!  Keen gives a little sigh and brings the wooden ladder over to the wall.  18  KEEN (holding the ladder) We don!t need a perfect party, Sunny. We just need our friends.  19  SUNNY (frowning tight) But I want it to be perfect! Well, I!ll make my own sunshine in here! Just watch!  Sunny climbs up the ladder holding a big, soaking wet string of yellow flowers. She grunts and pushes the wet garland onto the cave wall. It goes SQUISH. It is heavy and sad like a wet sponge. DRIP. DRIP. DRIP. Cold rainwater falls from the wet garland … right onto Keen!s head at the bottom of the ladder. Keen blinks. The water runs right down his nose. Sunny freezes, staring down at him. She gasps loudly, her eyes going wide.  20  KEEN (wiping his eye, trying to smile) Oh… no sunshine today? Just a drop?  Instead of calming down, Sunny throws her hands in the air.  21  SUNNY Oh no! Muddy water splashes all over Keen!s toes.  22  KEEN Sunny, wait, it!s just a little water — 
 
 
(he looks down and wiggles his toes) Actually… don!t worry! I love to splash in muddy puddles!  Keen gives a happy little STOMP. SPLASH!  23  SUNNY (scrambling down the ladder) Muddy puddles?! Sunny, realizing everyone is watching, forces a huge, panicked smile. She tries to pretend it!s all part of the plan.  26  SUNNY (sing-song, calling a square dance) Grab your partner, do-si-do!
